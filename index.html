Differences in U.S. Sex Ratios by County, 1820–1900

How to use this map

This map [UNDER DEVELOPMENT] shows differences in sex ratios by county. Counties shown in white had a very nearly equal ratio of females to males.

Map by Lincoln Mullen. Code available on GitHub, licensed MIT. Suggestions and criticism welcome: lincoln@lincolnmullen.com.

Citation

If you use this map in your research, I would appreciate a citation. Here is the suggested form:

Lincoln Mullen, "Differences in U.S. Sex Ratios by County, 1820–1900," interactive map, http://lincolnmullen.com/projects/sex-ratios/.

Explanation of Census data

The U.S. Census data and shapefiles for these maps comes from Minnesota Population Center, National Historical Geographic Information System, version 2.0 (Minneapolis, MN: University of Minnesota, 2011).
